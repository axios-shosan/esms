Saturday 19th March 2022

Qu’est ce que Computer science for high schoolers !

C’est un événement de deux jours visant à offrir des activités informatiques ludiques et éducatives pour les lycéens afin de se familiariser avec les concepts de base de l'informatique, qu'ils envisagent ou non de poursuivre une carrière dans ce domaine.

Objectifs de l'événement:
  • Initiation à l’informatique
  • Booster votre confiance et persévérance
  • Rendre l’informatique amusante
Activités incluses dans l'événement:
  • INITIATION A LA ROBOTIQUE
  • LEÇONS DE PROGRAMMATION
  • CONSTRUCTION D'ORDINATEURS

FAQ

Who can join Computer Science For Highschoolers ?
+

Since computer science is a scientific related domain, highschoolers of scientific fields are the most appropriate ones to join.

Do I need any prerequisites to join ?
+

No, CSFH's main goal is to introduce you to the world of computer science. All you need is motivation and curiosity.

How to join us ?
+

To join the conferences and the workshops, all you need to do is to fill the form that you will find in the website.

When will it take place ?
+

CSFH's first session will take place March 19th, 2022. The next session 2 weeks after.

What will I get from joining CSFH ?
+

CSFH will give you a great overview about the computer science field. By the end, you will be able to create some cool programs and machines and most importantly, you will have a better vision of what you want to become in the near future.

Any other questions ?
+
